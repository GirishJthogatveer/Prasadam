Krishna Prasadam

The Love Of Krishna

Prasadam literally means “Mercy” and devotees of ISKCON use this term to describe pure vegetarian food that has been offered to Lord Krishna. ..

However, when the food is prepared for the pleasure of Krishna. He accepts the love and devotion present in the offering and removes all sins from it.
